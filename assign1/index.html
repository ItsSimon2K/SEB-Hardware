Our Company's Services

Nationwide Delivery

Nationwide delivery are provided for all orders. Delivery fee will be charged based on the delivery location and weight of parcel. For orders above RM5000, free delivery will be provided.

Pick up at your Convenience

For those who prefer to pick up their parcels themselves can do so at any of our branch. The parcels will be kept at our place until it is picked up so you do not have to worry about it going missing.

After Sales Service

We provide after sales service so customers can call and enquire about their products if needed. Besides that, we provide repairing services for machines. The repairing fee depends on the type of repair and spare parts required. For those machines under warranty, repair service is free of charge.
