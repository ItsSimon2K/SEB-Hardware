Our Company

SEB Hardware is established in Kuching, Sarawak in 2020, with the primary responsibility of providing sales, support, service and spare parts (4S) for the local industries. SEB hardware is a hardware company that offers a wide range of high quality products and services for both commercial and industrial projects. The company comprises a team of highly-skilled and talented professionals in tune with current trends.

Objectives of SEB Hardware

The objectives of SEB Hardware are:

  • To increase the exposure of the company all around the world
  • To provide our customers with products and services at a competitive price
  • To strengthen customer’s knowledge by providing information on equipment and tools
